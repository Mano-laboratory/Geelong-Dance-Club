About Me

Founder and lead instructor of the Geelong Dance Club, passionate about bringing the community together through movement, rhythm, and creativity. Experienced in choreographing and teaching multiple dance styles, including hip-hop, contemporary, and freestyle, to dancers of all ages and skill levels. Skilled at designing engaging workshops, structuring classes for progressive learning, and creating a welcoming, high-energy environment that encourages participation and growth. Adept at event organization, social media promotion, and building a vibrant community of dance enthusiasts. Committed to fostering confidence, teamwork, and self-expression through dance while maintaining a safe and inclusive environment. Enthusiastic about connecting with local schools, organizations, and community groups to expand access to dance education and performance opportunities. Driven to deliver high-quality, fun, and inspiring experiences that promote physical wellness, artistic expression, and community engagement.
